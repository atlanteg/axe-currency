FIXXE — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 July 2026

FIXXE is a currency converter for Android and the web (PWA). This policy explains what data the app handles. Short version: FIXXE does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Data we collect

None. The app has no accounts, no sign-in, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking. We do not collect your name, email, location, contacts, device identifiers, or usage data.

Data stored on your device

Your preferences — the list of currencies, their order, decimal precision, chosen rate source, and interface language — are stored locally on your device only (Android SharedPreferences / browser localStorage). They never leave your device and are not sent to us or anyone else. Removing the app deletes them.

Network requests

To show exchange rates, the app fetches public mid-market rate data from these third-party services. Only a standard rate request is made; no personal data is included:

  • ExchangeRate-API — exchangerate-api.com
  • Fawaz Ahmed currency-api (jsDelivr / Cloudflare)
  • Frankfurter — data from the European Central Bank

These providers may log standard technical request information (such as IP address) under their own privacy policies, as any website does. FIXXE does not control or receive that data.

Permissions

The app requests only Internet access, used solely to fetch exchange rates.

Children

FIXXE does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new date.

Contact

For any question about this policy, please use the “Contact developer” option on the app’s Google Play listing.
